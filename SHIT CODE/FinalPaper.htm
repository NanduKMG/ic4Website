Final Paper Submission

Accepted papers should be revised incorporating the review comments and then format verification is to be done through PDF eXpress.

The procedure for PDF eXpress verification is listed below.

1
 Access the IEEE PDF eXpress site at http://www.pdf-express.org
a.
 Click "New Users - Click Here".
b.
 Enter 31132X for the Conference ID, your email address, and choose a new password. Continue to enter information as prompted.
c.
 Check that the contact information is still valid, and click "Submit".
2
 For each conference paper, click "Create New Title".
3
 Enter identifying text for the paper (title is recommended but not required)
4
 Click "Submit PDF for Checking" or "Submit Source Files for Conversion"
5
 Indicate platform, source file type (if applicable), click Browse and navigate to file, and click "Upload File". You will receive online and email confirmation of successful upload
6
 You will receive an email with your Checked PDF or IEEE PDF eXpress-converted PDF attached. If you submitted a PDF for Checking, the email will show if your file passed or failed.

Click here for printable version of Procedure for PDF eXpress verification

Final paper after verification is to be uploaded through EDAS Conference Management System. To submit the final paper login to EDAS website.

Please contact the conference secretariate by email for any problem faced during paper submission.
