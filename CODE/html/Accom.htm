Travel and Accommodation

Delegates are to make their own arrangement for travel and accommodation. However, the conference committee has arranged low cost accommodation for limited number of delegates on a twin sharing basis near the college campus.

The weather in Thiruvananthapuram during December will be pleasant and the temperature will be in the range of 20 to 30 degrees. Winter clothing is not normally required. However, temperature level at hill stations in Kerala will be low during December.

Thiruvananthapuram is a historic city and is well connected by rail and road network. The Sreepadmanabha Swami Temple and Kovalam beach are important tourist attractions in the city.

The conference venue is 1.5 km away from the Trivandrum Central Railway Station and 6 km away from the the international airport.

Delegates may have the following accommodation options.

Hotel Residency Tower (Conference hotel)

Special discounted rates are offered for the delegates of ICCC2015 as below.

Presidency suite - Rs. 6500 inclusive of tax
Delux Room - Rs. 4000 inclusive of tax
Executive Room (single occupancy) - Rs. 3500 inclusive of tax
Executive Room (double occupancy) Rs. 3800 inclusive of tax

Delegates may book directly with the hotel. Ask for 1CCC2015 rates at the time of booking.

Maria Rani Center (1.5 km away from College of Engg. and 10 km away from the Conference Venue)

Accommodation is arranged at Maria Rani Center near College of Engineering for a limited number of delegates. This is a retreat center managed by Christian Priests. Accommodation is on twin sharing basis and breakfast and dinner would be available from a common menu. Transport facility to the conference venue will be arranged by the ICCC 2015 management for the delegates. The rate for three day accommodation (from December 12 evening to December 15 morning) is Rs. 1200/- per delegate. This amount will cover cost of stay, food and conveyance to the conference venue. The rate will be the same even if the delegate stays for a shorter period.

Delegates who wish to avail this accommodation should book with the conference management. Please fill in the accommodation request form and send it to the Organising Secretary along with a DD for Rs. 1200/-. Alternately bank transfer for the same amount may be done in favour of ICCC 2015 and details along with accommodation request can be sent to support.iccc2015@cet.ac.in. Conformation will be intimated by Email.
